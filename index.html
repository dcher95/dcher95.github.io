I'm a PhD student at Washington University in Computational Data Sciences, where I have the honor of being an AI-ACCESS fellow. I'm working in the Multimodal Vision Research Laboratory led by Dr. Nathan Jacobs.

I worked as a Data Scientist tackling geospatial problems for 6 years prior to starting my PhD. My work focused on defining geospatial risk related to where people live and drive through the development and implementation of machine learning and statistical algorithms. In addition to geospatial modeling, I spent some time working on aerial imagery models. During that time I received my MS in Data Science from Worcester Polytechnic Institute.
